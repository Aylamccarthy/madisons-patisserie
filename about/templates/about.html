About Us

Welcome to Madison's Patisserie, where love, passion, and creativity blend seamlessly to create the most delectable baked treats for you and your family. We are more than just a bakeshop; we are a family-owned establishment deeply rooted in the heart of Cork.

Our Story
Our story started 8years ago when Madison - the founder and owner of Madison's Parisserie would make cakes and sweet treats for family and friends. Before long, she started getting requests from friends and family. And the rest is history, what started as a small family affair blossomed into Madison's Patisserie, a beloved bakeshop known for its mouthwatering pastries, cakes, and confections.

Our Philosophy
At Madison's, we believe in the power of simple, high-quality ingredients. We source our flour, sugar, butter and other essential ingredients from local farmers and suppliers, ensuring freshness and authenticity in every bite. Our skilled bakers, trained in both time-honored techniques and innovative trends, meticulously craft each item, infusing them with a touch of homemade warmth.

Our Commitment
Customer satisfaction is at the core of our values. We strive to create an inviting atmosphere where you can indulge in the sweet pleasures of life. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, sharing a moment with loved ones, or simply craving a delightful dessert, Madison's is the place where memories are made sweeter.
Thank you for being a part of our story. Join us on this delectable journey, and let's continue to spread joy, one bite at a time.
