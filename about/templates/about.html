About us

Welcome to Madison's Patisserie, where love, passion, and creativity blend seamlessly to create the most delectable baked treats for you and your family. We are more than just a bakeshop; we are a family-owned establishment deeply rooted in the heart of Cork.

Our Story
Our story started 8years ago when Ayla - the founder and owner of Madison's would make cakes and sweet treats for family and friends. Before long, she started getting requests from friends and family. And the rest is history, what started as a small family affair blossomed into Madison's Patisserie, a beloved bakeshop known for its mouthwatering pastries, cakes, and confections.

Our Legacy
Baking is more than just a profession for us; it's a cherished family tradition. Passed down from generation to generation, our recipes are a testament to the love and dedication of our family. Each bite of our treats carries the essence of our heritage, making every customer a part of our extended family.

Our Philosophy
At Madison's, we believe in the power of simple, high-quality ingredients. We source our flour, sugar, butter and other essential ingredients from local farmers and suppliers, ensuring freshness and authenticity in every bite. Our skilled bakers, trained in both time-honored techniques and innovative trends, meticulously craft each item, infusing them with a touch of homemade warmth.

Community Engagement
Being a family-owned bakeshop, we understand the importance of community. We actively participate in local events, sponsor school programs, and contribute to charitable causes within Cork. Our goal is to not only serve delightful treats but also to be a positive force within our community.

Our Commitment
Customer satisfaction is at the core of our values. We strive to create an inviting atmosphere where you can indulge in the sweet pleasures of life. Whether you're celebrating a milestone, sharing a moment with loved ones, or simply craving a delightful dessert, Madison's is the place where memories are made sweeter. Thank you for being a part of our story. Join us on this delectable journey, and let's continue to spread joy, one bite at a time.
